Design Hostel Management System:-
#Functional Requirements:
1. User Registration and Authentication:
   - Users (students, staff, and administrators) should be able to register and log in securely.
   - Password recovery and account management features should be included.
2. Room Allocation:
    - The system should allow administrators to allocate rooms to students based on availability and preferences.
    - Students should be able to view their room assignments and request changes if necessary.
3. Fee Management:
    - The system should track hostel fees, including payment status and due dates.
    - Students should be able to make payments online and view their payment history.
4. Maintenance Requests:
    - Students should be able to submit maintenance requests for their rooms.
    - Administrators should be able to track and manage these requests.
5. Visitor Management:
    - The system should allow students to register visitors and track their entry and exit times.
    - Administrators should be able to monitor visitor activity.
6. Reporting and Analytics:
    - The system should generate reports on room occupancy, fee collection, maintenance requests, and visitor activity.
    - Administrators should have access to analytics to help with decision-making.
